Frequently Asked Questions

BuildLet allows you to be a part owner in apartment & hostel buildings.

What is the minimum amount I can invest?

Our low minimum investment gives you the flexibility to invest the right amount, at the right time, to meet your goals.

You can invest a minimum of ₵100 & above as many times as you can. This is especially convinient for people that can spare ₵100 and upwards every month

What does Pending, Verified and Matured means?

As soon as you invest our system verifies and validate your investments with the tag "Verified"

An investment with the tag "Pending" means we have not yet receive the amount you invested

Investments in your "Matured" are investments which have reach their dividend payment period (A year)

How does the investors earn?

Real estate allows investors to accrue a unique mix of both long-term appreciation and income potential.

The value of the buildings we own increases over time combined with renturns from rent is how our investor's money grow

How do I receive my dividends at the end of the year?

At the end of every year you will be given the option to withdraw your dividends

If you don't want to withdraw it that year, it will be reinvest together with your total principal.
